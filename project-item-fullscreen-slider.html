Shake Up

“Shake up” it’s a vegan super food on powder special for those who don’t have the time to have breakfast everyday, but cares about their health. It is part of a complete program of fitness and health care named Instafit.
One of our client’s main objectives was to create habits on the consumers. That’s the reason why we decided to number each individual portion of powder so the users could organise their week and notice whenever they skip a meal.
